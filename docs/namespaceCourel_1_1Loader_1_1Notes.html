enum Courel.Loader.Notes.Visibility

Visibility type of a note.

Enumerator
Hidden 

A note with Hidden visibility will not be included in Courel.ScoreComposer.Composer.GetDrawableNotes, but it will be judged normally.

Fake 

A note with Fake visibility will not be included in Courel.ScoreComposer.Composer.GetDrawableNotes and will not be judged.

Normal 

A note with Normal visibility will be included in Courel.ScoreComposer.Composer.GetDrawableNotes and will be judged normally.
